Cinnamon Toast Crunch Cereal

Description

This is a cinnamon sugar cereal, perfect for anytime of the day.

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon Toast Crunch
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Pour the cinnamon toast crunch into your bowl.
  2. Pour your milk onto the cinnamon toast crunch.
  3. Use your spoon to eat and enjoy!
